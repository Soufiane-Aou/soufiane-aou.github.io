Introduction

Welcome to the QR Code Scanner & Generator app! This app is a versatile tool that allows users to scan and generate QR codes with ease. It comes equipped with the following features:

  • Scanning QR codes quickly and accurately.
  • Generating customized QR codes for various purposes.
  • Exporting QR codes as PDF files for easy sharing and printing.
  • Viewing a history of recently scanned or generated QR codes.

With our app, you can offer users a convenient way to interact with QR codes, whether for marketing, information sharing, or any other creative application.

Change Package Name

Changing the package name is a crucial step in reskinning your QR Code Scanner & Generator app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open your Android project in Android Studio.
  2. Navigate to the app folder, and locate the build.gradle file.
  3. Inside the build.gradle file, find the applicationId property.
  4. Change the applicationId to your desired package name.
  5. Sync your project with Gradle to apply the changes.

Change Icons

Customizing the app icons gives your QR Code Scanner & Generator app a unique look. Here's how:

  1. Prepare your icon images in various sizes (e.g., mdpi, hdpi, xhdpi, xxhdpi, xxxhdpi).
  2. In your Android project, locate the res folder and go to drawable folders.
  3. Replace the default icon files with your custom icon images for each resolution.
  4. Update the icon references in your app's manifest file (AndroidManifest.xml).
  5. Rebuild and run your app to see the new icons in action.

Change Screenshots

Updating app screenshots is essential for presenting your QR Code Scanner & Generator app. Follow these steps:

  1. Launch your app on a device or emulator in Android Studio.
  2. Take screenshots of the app's various screens and features.
  3. Replace the existing screenshots on your app's store listing with the new ones.

Change App Name

Renaming your app is the final touch. Here's how:

  1. Open the strings.xml file located in the res/values folder.
  2. Update the app_name string resource with your desired app name.
  3. Save the file.
  4. Rebuild and run your app to reflect the new name.

Extract AAB (Android App Bundle)

To extract your Android App Bundle (AAB) for distribution or further customization, follow these steps:

  1. Open your Android project in Android Studio.
  2. Go to "Build" in the top menu and select "Build Bundle(s) / APK(s)." Then, click "Build Bundle(s)."
  3. Choose the "Build Bundle" option.
  4. Once the build process is complete, locate the AAB file in the specified output directory.
  5. You now have the AAB file ready for distribution.
